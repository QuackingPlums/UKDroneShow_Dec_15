#ukdroneshow @ NEC

December 5 & 6, 2015

Race format:

Separate tournament on Saturday and Sunday. 12 pilots per tournament. Three rounds of qualifying, 2 minute races, points awarded for most laps in shortest time.
Qualifying positions calculated from all rounds and used to seed the Semi-finals knockout bracket.
Winner of each Semi-final automatically qualifies for Grand-final, one further wildcard slot awarded to next fastest pilot across all three Semi-finals. Grand Final winner wins the tournament
